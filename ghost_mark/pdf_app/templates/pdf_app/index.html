How It Works

The Email Tracking Border feature converts your email to a 10-digit number using a reversible algorithm. This number is then used to create a unique border pattern on your PDF.

The right edge of each page will have a unique stepped pattern in the top 1/8 section, where each step is based on a digit from your converted email.

Our Invisible Watermarking feature embeds text in a color nearly invisible to the human eye, which can later be extracted using our special extraction tool.
